Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are the results from Can You Run It?

A: The results are based on the information you provide and the hardware scanned on your computer. It's recommended to verify the results with the game's official system requirements.

Q: How often should I run the system scan?

A: It's a good idea to run the scan whenever you're considering installing a new game or software to ensure compatibility.

Q: Can I use Can You Run It on mobile devices?

A: Currently, the service is designed for desktop and laptop computers.
